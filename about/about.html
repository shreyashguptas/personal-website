About Me

Hello! I'm Shreyash Gupta, an Associate Director of Analysis and Strategy at the University of Arkansas. My passion lies in building a healthy culture of delivering analytics to organizations that serve students on campus.

By night, I'm dedicated to democratizing financial power. I work on innovative solutions that allow professionals to invest without prior knowledge, enabling those who want to reap the benefits of long-term investing to do so free of cost.

My interests span from machine learning and data analysis to financial technology and educational innovation. I'm constantly exploring new ways to leverage technology for positive societal impact.
